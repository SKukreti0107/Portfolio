About Me

From a young age, I was fascinated by technology, with one of my favorites being the game mechanics that fueled countless hours of creative exploration. As an Iron Man fan, I was captivated by JARVIS, the AI. Around the 8th grade, my curiosity in computers and AI ignited, propelling me into a journey that began with crafting hardcoded chatbots. This passion drives me forward, eager to contribute and thrive in the ever-evolving landscape of technology.
